# Kel'Thuzad

## Phase 1 (100% - 0%)

Avoid the puddle dropped by [Oblivion's Echo](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=347291/oblivions-echo)

CC any [Frostbound Devoted](https://www.wowhead.com/npc=180840/frostbound-devoted) not being attacked

Swap every [Soul Fracture](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=348071/soul-fracture)

Kill [Soul Shard](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=357275/soul-shard) quickly

Interrupt [Piercing Wail](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=348428/piercing-wail) or use [Oblivion's Echo](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=347291/oblivions-echo)

After [Soul Shard](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=357275/soul-shard) is killed tank with [Soul Exhaustion](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=348978/soul-exhaustion) debuff pick up the shard

Have to pick up all 3 shards within a minute
