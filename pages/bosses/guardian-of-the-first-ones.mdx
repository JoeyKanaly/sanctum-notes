# Guardian of the First Ones

## Phase 1

Avoid line [Disintegration](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=352833/disintegration)

Avoid [Form Sentry](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=352660/form-sentry)

Swap on [Sunder](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=350732/sunder) frontal

One tank should get [Sunder](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=350732/sunder) other should get [Obliterate](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=355352/obliterate)

Other members should help soak [Obliterate](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=355352/obliterate)

Take boss to [Energy Core](https://www.wowhead.com/npc=176583/energy-core)
after one cast of [Purging Protocol](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=352538/purging-protocol)

Stay in the [Radiant Energy](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=352394/radiant-energy)

Run away from [Energy Core](https://www.wowhead.com/npc=176583/energy-core) when it does [Meltdown](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=352589/meltdown)

Soft enrage after third [Energy Core](https://www.wowhead.com/npc=176583/energy-core)
