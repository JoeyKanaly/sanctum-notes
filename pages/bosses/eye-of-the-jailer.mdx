# Eye of the Jailer

## Phase 1 (100% - 25%)

Avoid [Fractured Soul](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=350034/fractured-soul)

Avoid [Annihilating Glare](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=350763/annihilating-glare) by running to opposite side and using extra action button

Defensive for [Piercing Lens](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=350803/piercing-lens)

Swap on each [Deathlink](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=350828/deathlink)

### Heroic

Off tank grab [Stygian Abductor](https://www.wowhead.com/npc=176521/stygian-abductor)

Swap on two stacks of [Assailing Lance](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=348074/assailing-lance)

## Intermission (75%, 50%, 25%)

Each tank takes one [Deathseeker Eye](https://www.wowhead.com/npc=179942/deathseeker-eye)

Keep them seperated to avoid [Shared Suffering](https://ptr.wowhead.com/spell=351816/shared-suffering) (30yd minimum)

Avoid players with [Desolation Beam](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=350847/desolation-beam)

## Phase 2 (25% - 0%)

Boss gains stacks of [Immediate Extermination](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=348969/immediate-extermination) kill fast
