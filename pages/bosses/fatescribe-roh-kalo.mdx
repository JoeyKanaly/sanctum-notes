# Fatescribe Roh-Kalo

## Phase 1

Avoid lines of [Fated Conjuction](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=350355/fated-conjunction) around room they will also be mirrored to other side

Easiest way to avoid [Fated Conjuction](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=350355/fated-conjunction) is to stand next to and outside of one of the beams

Swap on [Invoke Destiny](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=351680/invoke-destiny)

Every swap boss gains 3 stacks of [Diviner's Probe](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=353603/diviners-probe) that he will auto attack with to deal massive magic damage needs to be mitigated possibly healer CDs

Run away from raid after getting hit with [Invoke Destiny](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=351680/invoke-destiny)
after 8 seconds [Shade of Destiny](https://www.wowhead.com/npc=179124/shade-of-destiny) will spawn

Kite [Shade of Destiny](https://www.wowhead.com/npc=179124/shade-of-destiny) keeping it 10yds away

### Heroic

Move boss after [Call of Eternity](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=350819/call-of-eternity) to avoid [Echo of Eternity](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=350826/echo-of-eternity)

## Phase 2 (85%, 60%, 35%)

Players will be knocked back

If marked with [Runic Affinity](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=354964/runic-affinity) you must rotate runes to the active spot

Ring will move clockwise with 1 player and counterclockwise with 2 players

Runes must be placed within 50 seconds

Kill [Fatespawn Monstrosity](https://www.wowhead.com/npc=180323/fatespawn-monstrosity) before it gets too many stacks of [Monstrosity's Boon](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=357141/monstrositys-boon)

Interrupt [Despair](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=357144/despair)

Kill [Fatespawn Anomaly](https://www.wowhead.com/npc=179010/fatespawn-anomaly) by moving them into
[Fatespawn Monstrosity](https://www.wowhead.com/npc=180323/fatespawn-monstrosity) making sure not to get overwhelmed by them

Avoid [Fate Fragment](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=353162/fate-fragment) balls moving around the platform

## Phase 3

Just phase 1 and phase 2 combined but only 30 seconds to move runes
