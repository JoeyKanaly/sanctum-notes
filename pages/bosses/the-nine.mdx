# The Nine

## Phase 1 (Kyra & Signe 100% - 15%)

Run away from [Wings of Rage](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=350365/wings-of-rage)

Interrupt [Formless Mass'](https://www.wowhead.com/npc=177407/formless-mass) [Siphon Vitality](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=350339/siphon-vitality)

Swap at three stacks of [Unending Strike](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=350202/unending-strike)

Run towards boss for [Reverberating Refrain](https://wowhead.com/spell=350385/reverberating-refrain)

Interrupt [Song of Dissolution](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=350287/song-of-dissolution)

Avoid lines from [Agatha's Eternal Blade](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=350031/agathas-eternal-blade)

Move boss out of [Annhylde's Bright Aegis](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=350157/annhyldes-bright-aegis)

## Phase 2 (Skyja 100% - 0%)

Kill Kyra and Signe quickly

Swap at three stacks of [Pierce Soul](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=350475/pierce-soul)
